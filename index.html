Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Welcome to Caliro AI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Collected via Google Sign-In for authentication
  • Google Account Information: Name and profile photo from your Google account
  • Google Account ID: Unique identifier for authentication purposes

Usage Information

  • Meal Photos: Images you upload are temporarily processed by Google's Gemini AI for nutritional analysis. Photos are NOT permanently stored on our servers.
  • Daily Scan Count: We track the number of free scans you've used (resets every 24 hours)
  • Subscription Status: Whether you have an active premium subscription

Device Information

  • Local Storage: Meal history and analysis results are stored locally on your device only

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and manage your account
  • Service Delivery: To provide nutritional analysis of your meal photos
  • Usage Tracking: To enforce free scan limits (3 per day for free users)
  • Subscription Management: To manage premium features and billing
  • Service Improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve our app

3. Data Storage and Processing

What We Store on Our Servers

  • Email address
  • Google Account ID
  • Daily scan usage count
  • Subscription status
  • Account creation date

What We Do NOT Store

  • Photos: Your meal photos are NOT permanently stored on our servers. They are only temporarily processed by Google's Gemini AI and then discarded.
  • Meal History: Your meal history is stored locally on your device only, not on our servers.
  • Payment Information: Handled securely by RevenueCat and Google Play, we never see your payment details.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google Sign-In

  • Purpose: User authentication
  • Data Shared: Email, name, profile photo
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: Analyze meal photos for nutritional information
  • Data Shared: Photos you upload (temporarily, not stored)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

RevenueCat

  • Purpose: Subscription management
  • Data Shared: Email address, subscription status
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • No permanent storage of sensitive photos

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view your data at any time in the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data
  • Correction: You can update your information through your Google account
  • Data Portability: You can export your meal history from your device
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the service at any time

7. Data Retention

  • Active Users: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Scan History: Resets automatically every 24 hours
  • Photos: Never permanently stored, immediately discarded after analysis

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

If you are accessing our service from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. By using our service, you consent to this transfer.
